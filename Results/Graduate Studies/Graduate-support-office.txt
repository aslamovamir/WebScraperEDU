            Welcome from Associate Dean Ann Cranston-Gingras, PhD
            
            
            
            
            
            
            
               
            
            
            Welcome to Graduate Education and the Graduate Support Office (GSO) in the College
               of Education! We take pride in the important role we play in the university's student
               success mission and are pleased to recognize the efforts and accomplishments of our
               graduate students.&nbsp;
            
            
            We encourage you to familiarize yourself with the information provided on our website
               and to make use of the resources offered. We are committed to recruiting and retaining
               diverse and highly-skilled students as highlighted in the College's Strategic Plan
               and to ensuring equity and quality as we work with you in achieving your goals.
