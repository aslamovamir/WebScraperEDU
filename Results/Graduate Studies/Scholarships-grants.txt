            Applying for College of Education Scholarships
            
            Apply for&nbsp;College of Education scholarships through the AwardSpring&nbsp;scholarship database. AwardSpring allows students to apply for USF scholarships by completing a brief
               questionnaire that shows a list of scholarship(s) they may qualify for based on responses.
               Some scholarships consider financial need and require that students complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).&nbsp;
            
            
            Apply Now
            
            For more information about the award opportunities listed below,&nbsp;please contact the
               College of Education Scholarship Office at eduscholarships@usf.edu.&nbsp;&nbsp;
