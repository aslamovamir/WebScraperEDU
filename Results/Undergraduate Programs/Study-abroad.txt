            Study Abroad Opportunities 
            
            
            
            
            There are many opportunities for students to integrate an international education
               or study abroad program into their experience at USF. Learn more about Education Abroad
               programs hosted by College of Education faculty by exploring the programs listed below.&nbsp;
            
            
            
            
            Summer 2022 Education Abroad Programs
            
            
               
            
            
            USF Costa Rica Studying and Teaching Abroad
            
            Program Dates: June 4-17, 2022
            
            
            Interested in participating in this program? Join us at a virtual information session
                     on Thursday, October 7 at 5:30 pm. For more information, contact Dr. Jennifer Jacobs
                     at jjacobs8@usf.edu.&nbsp;&nbsp;
            
            
            USF’s two-week cultural program in Costa Rica is designed to accelerate and enhance
               students’ knowledge of education in Costa Rica. The program features an immersion
               into Costa Rican culture while teaching in K-12 classrooms and visiting cultural landmarks,
               accompanied by USF professors in pleasant facilities.
